About Me

Hi, I'm Sean. A dual citizen of the US and UK, I was born and raised in London, England, and have been a Florida resident since the age of 28. Now in the Sunshine State, I share my life with my wonderful wife and kids, trying to cultivate a healthy, fulfilling lifestyle.

My ambition is to do things that improve the world. My professional journey as a programmer began early, as I eagerly tinkered with my first computer at the age of 8. Fast forward to my late teens, and I found myself immersed in the fast paced city of London, working in the financial sector until I turned 27. Since then, I've transitioned to remote work, as both a senior engineer and a leader, and regular contributor to the open source community. I also take pride in contributing to charitable causes.

When I'm not doing those things, I enjoy driving, playing outdoor basketball, and spending time with my family and friends. I'm a Christian and enjoy attending church, working on self improvement, and helping those around me.

Thank you for stopping by.
